Japanese Curry Classic

Description

Japanese curry is a flavorful and comforting dish made with a rich and mildly spicy sauce, typically served over rice. It often includes ingredients like tender meat, vegetables, and a curry roux that thickens and adds depth to the sauce.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound chicken thighs or beef, cut into chunks
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, peeled and sliced
  • 2 potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 3 tablespoons curry powder or curry roux (store-bought)
  • 4 cups chicken or beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooked rice for serving

Steps:

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and cook until translucent.
  2. Add the chicken or beef to the pot and cook until browned on all sides.
  3. Add the carrots and potatoes, stirring to combine with the meat.
  4. Pour in the broth and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low and simmer until the meat and vegetables are tender, about 20-30 minutes.
  5. Add the curry powder or curry roux to the pot and stir until fully dissolved. Simmer for an additional 10 minutes, until the sauce thickens.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve the curry over cooked rice.
